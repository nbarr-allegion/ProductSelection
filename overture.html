A cloud collaboration platform for the design and specification of door hardware.

Allegion's Overtur™ is a cloud-based suite of tools where project team members can come together to collaborate on specifications and the security design of doors and openings. These tools provide a centralized place to capture and maintain door hardware requirements and decisions, with easy options to push that information back to the design tools (e.g. Revit).

Review Designs Online

  • Organize and coordinate data for improved collaboration
  • See your designs in a live environment
  • Easily capture decisions and requirements about your project's hardware
  • Choose the plan viewing mode that fits your needs
  • Review and make collaborative changes in real time
